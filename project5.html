Multiplayer chat room via Steam with UE4

Introduction

This is a simple chat room where players can make their profiles, choose their characters, create, join rooms and chat with each others. Host has the power to setup rooms, kick players and transfer players to new maps.

Source project is available on Github: GitHub

Screenshots

A screenshot of the GameMode_BP. Custom Events are either 'Run on Server' or 'Run of Client'.

Players have connection to Steam and thus other players' rooms.

Player and host chatting.

So far this project was made mostly with the blueprint system. I am current on another multiplayer project but all classes are made from C++. Gameplay physics like shooting/projectile will also be tackled and handled
