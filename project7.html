GAMEPLAY ABILITY SYSTEM MINIGAME - ABILITY DRIVEN ARKANOID

Introduction

This is a very light weight mini game that was made in 4 days with the Gameplay Ability System from Unreal Engine. The game showcases the usage of GAS for ability activation, attribute modification and gameplay effect application.

The game at it core is an Arknoid game where player controls the platform for bouncing and preventing the balls from falling off the screen. Ball deals damage to bricks upon collision and player will move to next stage when all brick are destroyed. Game is over when player has no balls left on screen.

    • Usage of Gameplay Ability System:

    Player ball attack power, platform movement speed, ball speed are control by attributes. These can be modified with ease via gameplay effects.

    Brick has attributes such as Shield, Health and Armor. We can now have multiple different type of brick from from changing these attribute values

    Execution calculation is used for damage. This allows complex damage calculation formular with different damage type dealing different damage to shield or armor. Energy damage deals extra damage to shield and Kinetic Damage deals extra damage to health that is protected by armor.

    Gameplay controls are ability driven. Everything other than movement are trigger by ability activation. Ability has their own cooldown and activation restriction with gameplay tags.

    Launch Ball: Lauch a ball, has cooldown of 5s, will not be activated if there are already 3 balls on the screen.

    Switch Ball Core: switch the type of damage of the ball from Kinetic to Energy and vice versa, no cooldown

    Ball Implosion: make all balls on the screen explode, dealing AoE damage to the surrounding brick. The damage type is depend on the current damage mode. 5s cooldown.

    Time dialation: Temporary slow down time, cooldown 5s

    Various pickup are used to enhance gameplay. Pickup up grant gameplay effect to player. Gameplay effects in turn grant new abilities. These abilities are override to be trigger upon given.

    Heavy Object: Ball speed slow down bto 20% for 5s

    Defender Mode: Bigger rocket platform for 15s. Montage is played via gameplay ability task.

    Unstable Core: All ball collision explode and deal AoE damage. 20s duration.

    Zero Sum Discharge: Ball against ball collision deal massive AoE damage in a wide radius. 30s duration.

    And some other attribute modification pickup such as: move speed increase, ball speed increase...etc
